What we do

Practical help at every step

We're at the start of this journey: for now we mostly share our own experience and guidance — and we keep adding new services step by step.

Navigation & diagnosis

Step-by-step guidance through an unfamiliar healthcare and benefits system.

Legal help

Connect with vetted lawyers on residence, disability status and benefits.

City directory

Clinics, NGOs, therapists and benefits — searchable by country and city.

Community
